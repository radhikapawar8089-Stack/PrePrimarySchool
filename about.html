About Our School

Jijamata school was established on 2017 with a vision to provide better educational facility in rural area. Jijamata Preprimary school Burli began as a community effort to bring joyful early learning to Burli. Our school welcomes young children from nearby homes with warm classrooms, caring staff, and a focus on basic skills, values, and confidence.

Our History

Founded to serve local families, our school has grown into a safe place where children build friendships, explore new ideas, and learn through play. We remain rooted in village values and committed to giving every child a strong start.

Teaching Philosophy

We believe children learn best when they are active and engaged. Our teaching centers on play-based, activity-based learning that encourages curiosity, social skills, and budding independence.

  • Hands-on games for early literacy and numeracy
  • Creative art, music, and movement activities
  • Positive encouragement and individual attention
