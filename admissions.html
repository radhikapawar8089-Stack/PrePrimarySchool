Admissions

We welcome children who are ready to begin their first steps in school life. Please review the age criteria and bring the required documents to complete admission.

Age Criteria

  • Playgroup: 3 to 4 years
  • Nursery: 4 to 5 years
  • Jr KG: 5 to 6 years
  • Sr KG: 6 to 7 years

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card or ID proof
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Admission fee receipt (if applicable)

School Timings & Admission Period

School hours: Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

Saturday hours: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

Admissions are open throughout the academic year. Please call or visit to reserve a seat.
